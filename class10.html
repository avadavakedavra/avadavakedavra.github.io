Common Questions

Questions about Class 10

If your question isn't here, WhatsApp us — we respond quickly.

01Do you teach both Kerala State Board and CBSE Class 10?

Yes — we have dedicated curriculum tracks for both Kerala State Board (SCERT) and CBSE (NCERT). The teaching approach is the same: concept-first, small batches, regular tests. The content is tailored specifically to your board's syllabus and exam pattern.

02Which board is better — Kerala State or CBSE?

It depends on your long-term goals. CBSE is aligned to NCERT, which is the base for JEE and NEET preparation — students going the Class 11/12 science route often find CBSE a smoother foundation. Kerala State Board is activity-based and well-regarded locally — and we ensure State Board students get the extra conceptual depth needed for higher secondary. Both are excellent choices.

03Can I join Class 10 mid-year?

Yes — contact us to check batch availability. We'll assess how far the batch has progressed and arrange catch-up sessions or recording access for any missed chapters.

04Do you teach Maths Standard and Maths Basic for CBSE?

Yes. We cover both Maths Standard and Maths Basic options for CBSE Class 10. We generally recommend Maths Standard for students planning to take Mathematics in Class 11 (PCM track) — contact us and we'll advise based on your goals.

05Does Class 10 coaching help prepare for Class 11?

That's exactly the point. We teach Class 10 with Class 11 in mind. Students who understand the foundations here — laws of motion, chemical bonding, coordinate geometry — find Class 11 far less overwhelming. It's not just about passing the board exam.

06What is the fee structure?

Fees are charged monthly. There is no registration fee. See the full breakdown on the Fee Structure page → or WhatsApp us for current rates.
