Common Questions

Questions about Class 12

If your question isn't here, WhatsApp us — we answer quickly.

01Can I join Class 12 mid-year?

Yes — contact us on WhatsApp to check seat availability. We will assess how far the batch has progressed and arrange for you to catch up through recordings and dedicated revision sessions.

02Do you teach Kerala Board or CBSE Class 12?

Both. The Class 12 core curriculum is essentially the same across both boards. We cover the full syllabus for either, and the competitive exam preparation (NEET / JEE) is board-agnostic.

03How are boards and NEET / JEE balanced?

We don't treat them separately. Every concept is taught with both applications in mind. The board syllabus and the entrance exam syllabus overlap extensively — we teach that overlap deeply and handle the divergences efficiently. Students don't need to divide their time between "board prep" and "NEET/JEE prep".

04How many mock tests are there?

Monthly full-length mock tests are scheduled throughout the year — pattern-specific for NEET (PCB) or JEE Mains (PCM). In addition, chapter tests follow each completed topic. As the exam approaches, the frequency of full-length mocks increases.

05What is the fee structure?

Fees are charged monthly. There is no registration fee. See the full breakdown on the Fee Structure page → or WhatsApp us for current rates.

06What if I miss a class?

All classes are recorded. Enrolled students can watch recordings any time via the Live Classes portal →. Doubt-clearing sessions are scheduled regularly so missed content can always be covered.
