Enrolment & Admissions

How do I enrol in Sehr Academy?

Contact us on WhatsApp (+91 95394 09586) or call us directly. We'll discuss the right batch for your class and subject combination, check seat availability, and confirm your enrolment.

Walk-in visits to our Vatanappally, Thrissur centre are also welcome during class hours.

What classes and subjects do you teach?

We cover:

  • Class 10 — Foundation (Maths, Science)
  • Class 11 & 12 — PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Maths) and PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Entrance Exam Prep — NEET and JEE Mains
How many students are in each batch?

We cap batches at a maximum of 5 students to ensure every student receives individual attention, personalised feedback, and direct interaction with the tutor.

Private (1-on-1) sessions are also available. See our pricing page for details.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, a one-time registration fee applies at the time of enrolment. This covers initial assessment, study materials, and batch setup. Registration fees are non-refundable.

Contact us for the current registration fee amount.

Classes & Schedule

How often are classes held?

Classes are conducted 5–6 days per week (one weekly rest day). Each session is a minimum of 2 hours.

The full academic calendar — including test dates, holidays, and live class schedules — is available in the Academic Calendar.

Do you offer online classes?

Sehr Academy primarily conducts offline classes at our centre in Vatanappally, Thrissur.

For enrolled students, live class recordings are accessible through the student portal. For special or exceptional circumstances, contact us on WhatsApp to discuss options.

What if I miss a class?

Enrolled students can access class recordings via the Live Classes portal. Additionally, doubt-clearing sessions are scheduled regularly to catch up on missed content.

We recommend students inform the tutor in advance if they need to miss a session.

Are there any tests or assessments?

Yes. Regular assessments are part of the curriculum:

  • Chapter tests — after completing each topic
  • Monthly mock tests — full-length, exam-pattern tests
  • Term exams — comprehensive assessments each term

Results are shared with students and parents via the student portal.

Fees & Refund Policy

How are fees structured?

Fees are charged on a per-student, per-hour basis and are collected monthly. Rates vary by class level and batch size.

See the full breakdown on our Private Classes Pricing page and the Tuition Fee Structure.

What is the refund policy?

Fees are collected monthly. Refunds are handled as follows:

  • Withdrawal before the 5th of the month — the remaining unused portion of the month's fee will be refunded.
  • Withdrawal after the 5th of the month — no refund is issued for that month.
  • Registration fee — non-refundable in all cases.

For exceptional circumstances, contact us directly on WhatsApp.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept payments via:

  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS)
  • Cash (at the Vatanappally centre)

A payment receipt is issued for every transaction.

Study Materials & Resources

Are study materials provided?

Yes. Enrolled students receive:

  • Formula sheets, chapter summaries, and mindmaps
  • Solved PYQ (Previous Year Question) packs
  • Chapter-wise tests and mock exams

A selection of free materials is also available in the Downloads section for all visitors.

How do I access student resources online?

Enrolled students can log in at Student Login to access their personal portal — including attendance records, syllabus progress, test results, and achievements.

Live class recordings and upcoming class schedules are in the Live Classes portal.
