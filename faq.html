Enrolment & Admissions
01 How do I enrol at SEHR Academy?

The easiest way is to WhatsApp us at +91 95394 09586. We'll discuss the right batch for your class and subject combination, check seat availability, and confirm your enrolment — usually within the same day.

Walk-in visits to our Vatanappally, Thrissur centre are also welcome during class hours.

02 What classes and subjects do you teach?

We offer coaching for:

  • Class 9 & 10 — Foundation in Maths and Science (State & CBSE boards)
  • Class 11 & 12 — PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Maths) and PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Entrance Exam Prep — NEET and JEE Mains
03 How large are the batches?

We keep batches deliberately small so every student gets individual attention, personalised feedback, and direct access to the tutor. Less crowd management — more actual teaching.

Private 1-on-1 and small group sessions are also available. See our Private Session Pricing →

04 Is there a registration fee?

No — there is no registration fee at SEHR Academy. You simply pay the monthly tuition fee from your first month, and nothing else upfront.

Just contact us on WhatsApp to confirm your place, and you're in.

Classes & Schedule
05 How often are classes held?

Classes run 5–6 days per week with one weekly rest day. Each session is a minimum of 2 hours.

The full schedule — test dates, holidays, live class timings — is in the Academic Calendar →

06 Are classes online or offline?

SEHR Academy runs primarily offline at our centre in Vatanappally, Thrissur. Face-to-face learning with immediate doubt-clearing is core to how we teach.

For enrolled students, live class recordings are accessible via the student portal. For exceptional circumstances, reach out on WhatsApp.

07 What if I miss a class?

All classes are recorded. Enrolled students can access recordings via the Live Classes portal → at any time.

Regular doubt-clearing sessions are also scheduled to help students catch up. We recommend informing the tutor in advance when possible.

08 Are there tests and assessments?

Yes — assessments are built into the curriculum at every level:

  • Chapter tests — after each topic is completed
  • Monthly mock tests — full-length, exam-pattern papers
  • Term exams — comprehensive assessments each term

Results are shared via the student portal. Progress is tracked in the Syllabus Tracker →

Fees & Refund Policy
09 How are fees structured?

Fees are charged monthly, based on your class level and number of subjects. No hidden charges — what we quote is what you pay.

See the full breakdown: Tuition Fee Structure → and Private Session Pricing →

10 What is the refund policy? Important

No refunds are issued once admission is taken. All fees paid are non-refundable under any circumstances.

Please make sure you are fully committed before enrolling. If you have any questions before joining, reach out on WhatsApp — we're happy to clarify everything upfront.

11 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • UPI — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm
  • Bank transfer — NEFT / IMPS
  • Cash — at the Vatanappally centre

A payment receipt is issued for every transaction.
