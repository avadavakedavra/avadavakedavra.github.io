SSLC Exam

Know the exam, ace the exam

Understanding the Kerala Pareeksha Bhavan SSLC exam pattern is the first step to scoring A+ consistently.

80
Marks per Subject
Theory exam

Each SSLC subject carries 80 marks for the board exam (theory) plus 20 marks for internal/continuous evaluation — totalling 100 per subject.

A+
Grading Scale
Kerala Board

Grades: A+ (90–100%), A (80–89%), B+ (70–79%), B (60–69%), C+ (50–59%), C (40–49%), D (30–39%), E (below 30%). Our focus: A+ in every subject.

5
Compulsory Subjects
For SSLC pass

Students must pass all five compulsory subjects (Mathematics, Science I, Science II, English, Social Science) to clear the SSLC examination.

FAQ

Common questions about SSLC coaching

Quick answers to what parents and students ask most about our Class 10 SSLC programme.

01When should a student join for SSLC coaching?

Ideally at the start of Class 10, so we can build chapter by chapter from June. However, we also accept students mid-year — we run a quick diagnostic to identify gaps and catch up effectively.

02Do you teach in Malayalam medium or English medium?

Both. We explain concepts in the medium the student is most comfortable in — Malayalam medium or English medium. The SCERT syllabus and textbooks remain the same for both.

03How many subjects can a student take at SEHR Academy?

Students can join for one subject or all five — Maths, Physics & Chemistry, Biology, English, and Social Science. We recommend all core subjects for the best board exam results.

04Do you use the SCERT textbook directly?

Yes — our teaching is aligned to SCERT Kerala textbooks chapter by chapter. We also supplement with our own structured notes, activity worksheets, and a bank of previous SSLC board questions.

05What is the fee for Class 10 SSLC coaching?

Tuition fees are charged monthly, per subject. There is no joining fee or registration fee. See the fee structure page for current rates, or message us on WhatsApp for details.
