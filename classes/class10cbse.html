Mathematics

Standard or Basic — we teach both

CBSE offers two Mathematics papers at Class 10. We coach students for either — and help them choose the right one for their goals.

Mathematics Standard
For students continuing Maths in Class 11
Required for PCM track — JEE Mains preparation
  • Full NCERT syllabus at higher difficulty level
  • Eligibility to take Mathematics in Class 11 and 12
  • Essential for JEE Mains and engineering entrance preparation
  • Stronger foundation for Class 11 coordinate geometry & calculus
We recommend Standard for any student planning PCM in Class 11 or considering JEE.
Mathematics Basic
For students not taking Maths beyond Class 10
Commerce, Arts, and PCB tracks without Maths
  • Same NCERT chapters, moderate difficulty level
  • Does not allow Maths as a subject in Class 11
  • Good choice for students pursuing Commerce, Arts, or PCB without Maths
  • Less exam pressure while maintaining sound fundamentals
Students who later change their mind can appear for the Compartment exam to convert to Standard.

Common Questions

Questions about CBSE Class 10

If your question isn't here, WhatsApp us — we respond quickly.

01Do you teach both Maths Standard and Maths Basic for CBSE?

Yes — we coach students for both options. We generally recommend Maths Standard for any student planning to take Mathematics in Class 11 (PCM track) or considering JEE. Maths Basic suits students heading to Commerce, Arts, or PCB without Maths. We'll advise based on your goals.

02Does CBSE Class 10 coaching at SEHR Academy help with Class 11 preparation?

That's exactly the point. We teach Class 10 with Class 11 in mind — especially for CBSE students heading into PCM or PCB. The concepts covered here (electricity, chemical bonding, coordinate geometry, trigonometry) are the direct prerequisites for Class 11 Physics, Chemistry, and Maths. Students who understand them deeply don't get overwhelmed in Class 11.

03Can I join CBSE Class 10 mid-year?

Yes — contact us to check batch availability. We'll assess how far the batch has progressed and arrange catch-up sessions for any missed chapters so you're fully on track before exams.

04Are classes online or offline?

Classes are offline only, conducted in person at our centre in Vatanappally, Thrissur (Priyadarshini Road, Ganeshamangalam). Weekdays, 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM. Private transport facility is also available. We believe face-to-face teaching at this level makes a genuine difference.
